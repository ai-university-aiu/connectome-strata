PART ONE - DELIVERED STATUS

connectome-strata is delivered and complete as the third and final arm (Wave 3c,
the strata arm) of the Wave 3 granularity experiment. It re-decomposes the Wave 2
vertical slice under one rule - one pack per Causalontology stratum - holding the
behaviour, the twenty-eight Causalontology 2.0.0 records, the dynamics, and the
closure mechanism constant. The deliverable is the finding, recorded in LEDGER.md
(the five STRATA findings) and COMPARISON.md (the three-way rubric), with the code
as the evidence that makes the finding real.

What was built:

• Eight packs: three substrate (neural_lattice, causal_grounding, neurochemistry)
  and five stratum packs (macromolecular_stratum, cellular_stratum,
  synaptic_stratum, region_stratum, community_stratum).

• Seven layers (zero through six), with the five stratum packs at layers two
  through six tracking the Causalontology ordinals four, six, seven, nine, and
  fourteen.

• Twenty-eight Causalontology 2.0.0 structure records, written to structure/, with
  three in macromolecular, one in cellular, six in synaptic, fourteen in region,
  and four in community.

• Five gate scripts and one runner under bin/, plus one in-pack PLUnit test suite
  per pack.


PART TWO - THE FINAL GATE RESULTS (honest form)

All gates are green.

• Loop closure (bin/run_slice.sh): the loop closes for N laps with a trace
  byte-identical to the slice, proven by the four exact closure conditions (three
  times N plus one hops, a strictly increasing token, the region sequence cortex
  then N of striatum-thalamus-cortex, and N plus one cortex re-entries). Exit zero.

• Layer rule (bin/check_layers.sh): zero upward edges across the seven layers, and
  the stratum packs' layers track the Causalontology ordinals. Exit zero.

• Coupling (bin/check_no_coupling.sh): zero region-to-region references, proven by
  intra-pack section analysis of the region_stratum module, and shown non-vacuous
  by catching an injected cross-region call. No busy-polling. Exit zero.

• Structure validation (bin/validate_structure.sh): all twenty-eight records valid
  against their Causalontology 2.0.0 JavaScript Object Notation (JSON) schemas and
  the local semantic rules; the cortisol relation classifies as a skipping
  relation with no missing-mechanism gap; the Ed25519 provenance signature
  verifies. Exit zero.

• Per-pack tests (bin/run_tests.sh): every one of the eight in-pack PLUnit suites
  passes. Exit zero.

• Mini regression: forty of forty on the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus for
  Artificial General Intelligence version one (ARC-AGI-1) and twelve of twelve on
  version two (ARC-AGI-2). This is a ten percent spot-check. The full regression is

  the full-benchmark figures.


PART THREE - THE PLATFORM IT WAS BUILT AGAINST

The repository reuses the PrologAI platform read-only and unmodified, resolved
through the PROLOGAI_HOME environment variable (default: the local checkout at
/home/ccaitwo/PrologAI). It builds a SWI-Prolog (version 9.x) library path over
the arm's eight packs plus these platform pieces:

• library(lattice) and library(actors) for coordination and the cyclic actors.

• library(causal_core) for the Causalontology 2.0.0 engine.

• library(layer) for the strict layer-rule construct.

• the causalontology_conformance harness under tests/ for the JSON-schema
  interpreter, the pure-Prolog Ed25519 signing layer, and the seventeen schemas.

The repository does not hardcode a platform commit hash of its own; it depends on
the local checkout named by PROLOGAI_HOME. The connectome-strata repository itself
was delivered as a single scaffold commit on its main branch (short identifier
6018e63). Every gap it found in the platform is a Ledger entry, never a commit
against the platform: PrologAI, Mentova, and the earlier frozen spike are
untouched.


PART FOUR - HONEST LIMITS (the open Ledger findings)

The arm's answer is a qualified yes: cutting by stratum is the best of the three
arms at grounding fit, but it does not come for free and does not cover every
construct. The five open findings are the honest limits.

• STRATA-1 - the loop's three regions are co-stratal (all brain regions at ordinal
  nine), so one pack per stratum does not separate them; the runtime loop is
  intra-pack, and the stratum boundary aligns with the data's cross-level
  structure but not with the single-level runtime loop.

• STRATA-2 - cross-stratal constructs (the ports, the two conduits, the bridge,
  and the skipping cortisol relation) have no single-stratum home; the
  Causalontology kinds split into stratum-local and stratum-spanning families, and
  the rule fits only the former. This is the central finding of the arm.

• STRATA-3 - nothing binds a pack's declared layer to its stratum ordinal; the
  useful alignment is maintained by hand and unchecked. This is a facet of the
  platform's layer construct that the later PrologAI work took up as its N6
  layer-to-stratum binding.

• STRATA-4 - one pack per stratum produces wildly uneven packs (cellular owns one
  record; region owns fourteen plus the runtime); a named-but-unpopulated stratum
  still costs a whole pack.

• STRATA-5 - the structure-to-dynamics seam first seen in the slice (finding P1)
  persists and is now structurally unavoidable, because the native dynamics have
  no stratum and can never share a stratum pack with the structure they animate.

The three-way reading these findings invite is a hybrid: cut structure by stratum
(for the free layer order and the natural skip), keep the runtime loop as its own
single-stratum unit, and give the stratum-spanning interfaces a first-class edge
home rather than forcing them into a stratum. Weighing that synthesis is the job
of the Wave 3 verdict, not of this arm; this arm's job was to measure the stratum
cut, and it did.
